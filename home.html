"Business-to-Consumer"

B2C, or Business-to-Consumer, refers to the relationship between businesses and individual consumers. In this dynamic, companies focus on meeting the needs, desires, and preferences of individual customers. B2C businesses operate in a wide range of industries, offering a diverse array of products and services directly to consumers./p>

"Consumer-to-Consumer"

C2C, or Consumer-to-Consumer, refers to a business model where individual consumers engage in transactions with one another. In this model, individuals act as both buyers and sellers, using platforms or marketplaces to facilitate the exchange of goods or services.

"Business-to-Business"

B2B, or Business-to-Business, refers to the relationship between businesses where one business sells products or services to another business. Unlike B2C, B2B transactions involve catering to the needs of other businesses rather than individual consumers.
